About full stack conf

Set in Kigali, near Belgian Peacekeepers Memorial, Kigali City Tower and Kandt Hou a flat-screen TV, a sofa and desk. Guests at the apartment can enjoy a continental or an American breakfast. Makuza Peace Plaza offers a hot tub. If you would like to discover the area, skiing is possible in the surroundings. Kigali Centenary Park is 6 km from the accommodation, while MTN Centre Kigali is 10 km away.
